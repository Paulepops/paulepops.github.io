Paulepops!

I started "learning computers" in 1988 and haven't looked back. I've tried various programming languages (I'm a better debugger than coder), managed several databases of various types, and was heavily involved with general and Y2K software testing. But my real passion has always been the web. I started with HTML2 and CSS1, and have been hooked ever since. I'm a huge supporter of web standards and web accessibility.

Work History

21 Years!

I joined the Royal Australian Air Force straight out of school and learned the adminstration trade while transferring around the country. I finished by career working in Canberra on the Air Force's personnel database systems.

22 Years!

Straight out of the Air Force and into the Australian Public Service! After Y2K testing, I transferred to web administration, content management and database administration. I finished my second career improving website accessibility and programming the Community forum site for the Australian Taxation Office.

2 Years (and counting)

I've long since retired ... my son's moved away ... he called me up just the other day! But seriously, with apologies to Harry Chapin, I'm teaching myself all the web stuff I didn't have time to learn while I was working!
